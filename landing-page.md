## Landing Page Plan — Abie Maxey

### 1) Hero
- Headline: “UGC Videos + Web Development for Brands That Move”
- Subheadline: Digital nomad & software engineer helping brands grow with high-converting UGC and modern web builds.
- Primary CTA: “Book a Free Consult”
- Secondary CTA: “View Work”

- Proof: Client logos or quick stats (e.g., “100+ videos delivered”, “5 years in dev”).

### 2) About
- Short bio: Abie’s background as a software engineer + creator.
- Values: speed, authenticity, measurable growth.
- Personal touch: “Currently based in [city], traveling worldwide.”
- Supporting visual: collage of travel snapshots + behind-the-scenes creation.

### 3) Services
- UGC Video Packages
  - Concepts, scripting, filming, editing, hooks, captions, and variants.
- Web Development
  - Landing pages, full sites, performance optimization, SEO basics.
- Optional add-ons
  - Creative strategy, A/B testing, content repurposing.

### 4) Pricing
- Starter UGC: 3 videos / month
- Growth UGC: 8 videos / month
- Web Dev Sprint: single-page or multi-page site
- CTA: “Request Custom Quote” for mixed bundles.
- Note: “All packages include discovery call and revision rounds.”

### 5) Testimonials
- 2–3 quotes from brands or founders.
- Include short outcomes (e.g., “+28% CTR”, “launch in 10 days”).
- Photos or brand marks for credibility.

### 6) Footer
- Contact CTA: email + booking link.
- Socials: TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, GitHub.
- Quick links: About, Services, Pricing, Testimonials.
- Location note: “Working remotely worldwide.”

### 7) Style & Voice
- Tone: confident, friendly, creator-first.
- Visuals: warm travel palette, bold typography, kinetic motion cues.
- Emphasis: balance creator energy with engineer credibility.
